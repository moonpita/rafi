High-quality quick-drying premium enamel mirror metallic KUDO ® with an updated formula is designed for decorative painting of metal, wood, plastic, gypsum and ceramic surfaces.

It has a bright and rich metallic luster. Spray paint can be easily applied to hard-to-reach surfaces. The enamel gives an excellent visual effect, but does not have a very high abrasion resistance (more details in the application). It is used for interior work.

Application
  • To avoid aerosol traces, it is recommended to protect surfaces that are not subject to painting.
  • For best results, apply the enamel at an ambient temperature of at least +10 ° C.
  • Shake the balloon vigorously for 2-3 minutes before use.
  • The enamel is applied to a clean, dry and non-greasy surface. To achieve the best results and obtain a more durable coating, it is recommended to apply enamel on pre-primed surfaces.
  • It is recommended to use "KUDO ® acrylic primer" for priming the surface. The enamel is applied from a distance of 25-30 cm in 1-2 layers with intermediate drying for 10 minutes at a temperature of + 20 ° C. The drying time "for peeling" at a temperature of +20 ° C is 15-20 minutes. The time of complete drying at a temperature of +20 ° C is 2 hours.
  • It is not recommended to apply enamel on surfaces that are subjected to regular mechanical stress. Applying varnish over enamel reduces the decorative effect of the coating.
Attention! At the end of the work, in order to avoid clogging of the spray head, turn the cylinder upside down and spray until the enamel stops flowing.
